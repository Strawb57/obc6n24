OBC 2024 Six Nations total points scored competition

Introduction

Welcome to the Old Barn Club (OBC) 2024 Six Nations total points competition!

This is your chance to test your knowledge to guess the total number of points scored by all the teams over the total of 15 games. There are 4 prizes for the closes entries.

So what are you waiting for make a guess for the total number of points!

France and Ireland start the 2024 6 Nations when they meet in France at the Orange Velodrome in Marseille on Friday the 2nd of February. :

Rules

1, Nearest the total points scored wins!

2, There are 4 prizes. £130 for 1st prize, £70 for 2nd prize, £45 for 3rd prize and £25 for 4th prize.

3, If two entries are closes to a prize then the next prize is included. i.e. if 2nd place is equal between 2 entries then (£70+£45) £115 will be split between the two entries. If the 4th prize is shared then the 2 entries will split the £25.

About

This website is under development and will support the OBC 2024 Six Nations total points competition. The Web app has been developed so that some parts can be reused again for future similar projects.

The website's spreadsheet should be updated after each round or day played. The daily update of will allow the contestants to view the results more frequently instead of waiting for a poster to be printed and pinned to the notice board.

The website originally included much more functionality but has now been simplified to just support the results/statistics and information links. Future 6 Nations versions will allow the administrator to update the required data realtime instead of waitng to the end of the day.

If you have any new requirements for the website give us a shout, hopefully they will be included ASAP.Sports_Rugby
